How Babies Are Made

When a mom and dad want to have a baby, a tiny cell from the dad, called a sperm, meets a tiny cell from the mom, called an egg.

This usually happens when adults share a special kind of hug called sex, where their bodies come very close together.

Inside the mom’s body, the sperm and egg join to start making a baby. The baby grows inside the mom for about nine months before being born.
